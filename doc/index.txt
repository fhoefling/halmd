HALMD documentation
===================

Welcome to HALMD, a high-precision molecular dynamics simulation of
Lennard-Jones fluids for NVIDIA CUDA devices.

For suggestions or bug reports, send a mail to
Peter Colberg <peter.colberg@physik.uni-muenchen.de>.
